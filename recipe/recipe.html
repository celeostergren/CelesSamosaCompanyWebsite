Beef Samosa Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 460 g ground beef
  • 1 medium red onion, diced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 1 cup water

Cooking Procedure

  1. Heat the oil in a large pan until hot, add the ground beef and cook for 5 minutes. Add onions and cook until the meat is brown.
  2. Add garlic, salt, pepper, coriander and cumin to the meat and cook for 2 minutes. Remove it from heat and let it cool.
  3. In a large bowl, mix the flour, salt, olive oli and water until the dough is well formed. Transfer it to a lightly floured surface. Leave it for 5 minutes until the dough is smooth and soft.
  4. Shape the dough in small triangular shapes and fill in with the meat and fold.
  5. In a large deep pan, heat the oil to 180 degrees celcius and carefully deep the samosas in the pan. Fry for about 6 minutes while flipping until light golden brown and serve.
